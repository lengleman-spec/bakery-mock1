About Us

Brookside Bakery began in Pittsburgh, PA in 2021 with a simple idea: share the kind of homemade treats our family has always loved. We’re a family-run shop, and everything that comes out of our kitchen is baked fresh each day and hand-decorated personally — no shortcuts, no mixes, no machines, just real ingredients, busy hands, and a lot of heart.

We specialize in decorated cookies and custom cakes for all of life’s special occasions. Birthdays, weddings, baby showers, or “just because” — we love creating sweets that feel personal and memorable. None of our bakers have formal training; every recipe, every design, and every skill was learned through family generations. What started as family traditions became a small community bakery built on creativity, curiosity, and a love for sharing good food. At Brookside Bakery, we hope every bite feels like home.

Our Mission

At Brookside Bakery, our mission is to create pastries and custom treats that are as fresh, honest, and heartfelt as the family traditions they come from. We are committed to using fresh ingredients, baking everything 100% homemade, and crafting each cookie and cake with the same care we put into our own family table. We strive to bring joy to our community through beautifully made, freshly baked sweets that celebrate life’s special moments.

Our Promise

At Brookside Bakery, we’re committed to using only fresh, high-quality ingredients in every recipe. Each pastry is made with care, attention, and a genuine love for homemade baking. From the first mix to the final decoration, we put in the extra effort to ensure every treat is something we’re proud to share with you.
