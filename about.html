About Us

Brookside Bakery began in Pittsburgh, PA in 2021 with a simple idea: share the kind of homemade treats our family has always loved. We’re a family-run shop, and everything that comes out of our kitchen is baked fresh each day — no shortcuts, no mixes, just real ingredients and a lot of heart.

We specialize in decorated cookies and custom cakes for all of life’s special occasions. Birthdays, weddings, baby showers, or “just because” — we love creating sweets that feel personal and memorable. None of our bakers have formal training; every recipe, every design, and every skill was learned through family generations. What started as family traditions became a small community bakery built on creativity, curiosity, and a love for sharing good food. At Brookside Bakery, we hope every bite feels like home.
